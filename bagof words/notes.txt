in this case its difficult to understand the pattern 
so we use the tf(term frequency)-idf(inverse document frequency)

let take sentence 1 : "good boy"
         sentence 2 : "good girl"
         sentence 3 : "good boy girl"

in this case we can see that "good" is repeating in all the sentences so we can say that "good" is not a good word to differentiate between the sentences.
and there is no rare case or rare words in the sentences.
so we use tf-idf to solve this problem.

example:
cat eats food
bat eats food 
kalu eats food

so in this case of sentences we can see that "eats" and "food" are common words in all the sentences.
but the words "cat", "bat", "kalu" are unique to each sentence.


#the formula

tf (term frequency)= 
number of times the term appears in the document / total number of terms in the document
idf (inverse document frequency) = 
log(total number of documents / number of documents containing the term)
tf-idf = tf * idf
